About SafeVault

Empowering digital security with innovation, elegance, and strength.

Who We Are

SafeVault was founded by Priyanka and Aesha with a vision to make digital security both accessible and exceptional. Combining deep technical expertise with modern design principles, we aim to redefine the way individuals and teams protect their most sensitive data.

What We Do

  • End-to-end encryption for maximum data protection
  • Biometric authentication & two-factor security
  • Autofill tools for passwords and payment methods
  • Password health reports and breach alerts
  • Seamless encrypted syncing across devices
  • Secure data sharing for teams and families

Our Philosophy

Inspired by a blend of strength and elegance, SafeVault believes security doesn’t have to be boring. We bring a bold yet sophisticated approach to cybersecurity — one that empowers users without overwhelming them.
